I created two more buttons that allow the user to identify whether or not they got the trivia question right. If they did, I added a scoreboard that increments each time the correct button is pressed. If press the button that states you did not get the question right, the incorrect button will subtract one from the score. The new additions are designed to match/compliment the appearance of the "app" as well. The new paragraph will not be displayed until the "Show Answer" button is pressed.
